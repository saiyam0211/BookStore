Pillar of the Wolves

Lola Glass
4.9 (2.8k reviews)
420 pages

In a world where humans and wolves share an ancient bond, one girl discovers she holds the key to both species' survival. Pillar of the Wolves follows the extraordinary journey of Maya, a young woman who discovers she can communicate with wolves in ways no one has done for centuries. As dark forces threaten both human and wolf communities, Maya must embrace her unique abilities and unite two worlds before it's too late.
