The Scrawled Diary

Den
4.5 (1.8k reviews)

A haunting tale of mystery and self-discovery, The Scrawled Diary follows the journey of a young woman who discovers an old diary filled with cryptic messages and dark secrets. As she delves deeper into the mysterious entries, she uncovers a web of intrigue that spans generations and threatens to unravel everything she thought she knew about her own past.
